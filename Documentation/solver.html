How to use the Solver

To try to solve a level, load it and click on Solve at the top bar, or hit F9.

To cancel the solver, click on Stop Solver or hit F8 or ESC. If a solution is found, it will be placed on the message log.

When to use the Solver

The solver works best when the levels are relatively small, and the winning conditions are of the form all X on Y, no X or no X on Y.

In the future, maybe I'll add a form of automatic heuristic extraction into the compiler, to generate better search gradients and enhance the performance.
